Cheesy Onion Rings

On one hand, you have the gooey, golden brown mozzarella sticks that grow even stringier with each bite. These sticks are perfect when you dip them in a delicious marinara sauce. On the other, very few people can resist the temptation that a fantastic onion ring can bring. Just like mozzarella sticks, onion ringsâ€™ golden brown breadcrumb coating make them a star side at any restaurant or home.

Ingredients

  • 1 piece onion large sliced into thick rings
  • 8 Mozzarella cheese sticks
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1 cup Panko bread crumbs
  • 2 cups cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt fine

Recipe instructions

  • step 1

    Pair two slices of onion rings. One piece should be larger than the other and there should be a space in between the rings when the smaller piece is placed inside the larger one.

  • step 2

    Slice the mozzarella cheese in such a way that it fits the space in between the rings.

    Put the sliced cheese in between the two pieces of onions and secure by inserting a toothpick. Perform this step on all pairs of onion rings that you have.

  • step 3

    Heat oil in a deep cooking pot.

  • step 4

    Combine all dry ingredient mixture in a bowl. Mix well. Dredge all onion rings onto the dry ingredient mixture. Place over a wire rack. Set aside.

  • step 5

    Combine milk and egg. Mix well. Pour the mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Mix well until a better is formed.

  • step 6

    Dip the onion rings in the batter mixture. Put it back over the wire rack for the excess batter to drip.

  • step 7

    Coat with Panko breadcrumbs.

  • step 8

    Fry one side until it turns golden brown. Turn it over and do the same thing on the opposite side.

  • step 8

    Serve warm with marinara sauce. Share and enjoy!
